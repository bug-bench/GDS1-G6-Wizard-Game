## 1. 核心系统重构与新增功能 / Core System Refactoring & New Features

### 1.1 状态异常系统 / Status Effect System
在 `PlayerStats.cs` 中新增了底层的异常状态支持，所有状态均采用**“刷新机制”**（重复施加时刷新持续时间，不叠加数值），避免了海量伤害或负数移速的 Bug。
Added underlying status effect support in `PlayerStats.cs`. All effects use a **"refresh mechanism"** (re-applying refreshes the duration instead of stacking values) to prevent bugs like massive damage spikes or negative movement speed.
*   **燃烧 (Burn)**：真实持续伤害 (DoT)，不会触发受击闪白和无敌帧。
    *True Damage over Time (DoT). Does not trigger hit flashes or invincibility frames (i-frames).*
*   **减速 (Slow)**：百分比减速，持续时间结束后精准恢复。
    *Percentage-based speed reduction. Recovers accurately after the duration ends.*
*   **眩晕 (Stun)**：硬直状态，期间玩家无法移动、无法转向、无法施法（但会因惯性滑行）。
    *Hard crowd control. The player cannot move, rotate, or cast spells (but will slide due to inertia).*
*   **定身 (Root)**：禁锢状态，期间玩家无法移动，但可以正常转向和施法。
    *Immobilization. The player cannot move, but can still rotate and cast spells normally.*

### 1.2 通用陷阱与区域系统 / Universal Hazard & Area System (`HazardArea.cs`)
新增了一个高复用性的通用脚本，用于实现所有“留在地上的法术”。
Introduced a highly reusable generic script to handle all "ground-based spells".
*   **功能 (Features)**：挂载在带有 Trigger 的 Collider 上，即可配置进入瞬间伤害、持续秒伤 (DPS)、附加燃烧/眩晕/定身/减速等效果。
    *Attach to a Trigger Collider to configure instant enter damage, Damage per Second (DPS), and apply Burn/Stun/Root/Slow effects.*
*   **免疫机制 (Immunity Mechanism)**：支持 `ignoreCaster`，避免施法者自己踩中陷阱（如火焰冲刺留下的火路）。
    *Supports `ignoreCaster` to prevent the spellcaster from stepping on their own hazards (e.g., the fire trail left by Flame Dash).*

### 1.3 LOL 风格的数值系统 / LOL-Style Stats System
重构了 `PlayerStats.cs`，为“吃豆人”玩法的地图道具做好了底层接口铺垫。所有属性均支持通过 `ModifyStat(string statName, float amount)` 统一调用。
Refactored `PlayerStats.cs` to lay the groundwork for the "Pac-Man" style map pickups. All stats can be modified using the unified `ModifyStat(string statName, float amount)` interface.
*   **Health (生命值)**：无上限，吃道具直接叠加。
    *No cap, stacks infinitely when picking up health items.*
*   **Speed (移动速度)**：增加移速，但底层物理（`PlayerController`）会导致刹车距离变长（惯性变大）。
    *Increases movement speed, but underlying physics (`PlayerController`) will increase stopping distance (higher inertia).*
*   **Strength (法术强度)**：直接附加到法术的基础伤害上。
    *Added directly to the base damage of spells.*
*   **Defense (护甲/防御力)**：引入 LOL 护甲公式 `实际伤害 = 原始伤害 * (100 / (100 + 防御力))`。
    *Introduced the LOL armor formula: `Actual Damage = Raw Damage * (100 / (100 + Defense))`.*
*   **Size (法术体积)**：倍率加成（`sizeMultiplier`），吃道具后发射的火球、火圈等会变大。
    *Multiplier (`sizeMultiplier`). Spells like fireballs and fire circles will become larger after picking up size items.*
*   **Focus (冷却缩减)**：百分比减 CD（最高 90%）。作为 Trade-off，获得减 CD 时会小幅扣除法强 (Strength)。
    *Percentage Cooldown Reduction (capped at 90%). As a trade-off, gaining CDR will slightly reduce spell power (Strength).*

## 2. 魔法流派技能实现 / Magic School Spell Implementations

基于上述底层系统，我们对三大流派的技能进行了重构和实现：
Based on the underlying systems above, we refactored and implemented the spells for the three magic schools:

### 🔥 火系流派 (Fire)
*   **火球术 (Fireball)**：在原 `SpellProjectile` 基础上，通过 Inspector 配置 `Apply Burn Damage`，命中后附加燃烧状态。
    *Based on the original `SpellProjectile`, configured `Apply Burn Damage` in the Inspector to apply the Burn status on hit.*
*   **火焰冲刺 (Flame Dash)**：新增 `FlameDashSpell.cs`。按住副键冲刺，并在身后高频生成存活 2 秒的 `HazardArea`（火路），敌人踩中会燃烧。
    *New `FlameDashSpell.cs`. Hold the sub-button to dash, frequently spawning a 2-second `HazardArea` (fire trail) behind. Enemies stepping on it will burn.*
*   **火焰结界 (Flame Circle)**：纯预制体拼装。利用 `UtilityAreaSpell.cs` 在脚下生成一个大范围的 `HazardArea`，造成高额 DPS 和燃烧。
    *Prefab assembly only. Uses `UtilityAreaSpell.cs` to spawn a large `HazardArea` at the feet, dealing high DPS and Burn.*

### ❄️ 冰系流派 (Ice)
*   **寒冰射击 (Frost Shot)**：基于霰弹枪 (`ShotgunSpell`) 魔改，子弹模型改为蓝色三角形，命中后附加百分比减速。
    *Modified from the shotgun (`ShotgunSpell`). Bullet model changed to a blue triangle, applying percentage Slow on hit.*
*   **冰霜滑行 (Ice Slide)**：新增 `IceSlideSpell.cs`。按住副键时，移动速度大幅提升，同时摩擦力 (`moveDeceleration`) 降至极低，实现难以拐弯的“溜冰”手感。
    *New `IceSlideSpell.cs`. Holding the sub-button greatly increases speed while dropping friction (`moveDeceleration`) to a minimum, creating a hard-to-turn "ice skating" feel.*
*   **寒冰陷阱 (Ice Trap)**：纯预制体拼装。利用 `HazardArea` 配置定身 (Root) 和重度减速效果，触发后生效。
    *Prefab assembly only. Uses `HazardArea` to configure Root and heavy Slow effects upon triggering.*

### ⚡ 雷系流派 (Lightning)
*   **闪电箭 (Lightning Bolt)**：常规直线极速弹道（取消了弹射逻辑以保证稳定性）。
    *Standard ultra-fast linear projectile (removed bounce logic to ensure stability).*
*   **闪现 (Blink)**：优化了现有的 `BlinkSpell.cs`，在落地后给自己附加 0.5 秒的眩晕 (Stun) 作为硬直惩罚。
    *Optimized the existing `BlinkSpell.cs`, applying a 0.5s Stun to the caster upon landing as a recovery penalty.*
*   **静电场 (Static Field)**：纯预制体拼装。利用 `HazardArea` 配置进入即眩晕 (Stun) 的效果。
    *Prefab assembly only. Uses `HazardArea` to configure a Stun effect upon entry.*

## 3. 视觉反馈优化 / Visual Feedback Optimization

### 3.1 护盾视觉重做 / Shield Visual Rework
*   **Reflect Shield**：将原本的“身前弧形盾”改为了“包裹全身的 360 度圆环”。
    *Changed the original "front arc shield" to a "360-degree full-body circle".*
*   **判定优化 (Hitbox Optimization)**：盾牌现在直接挂载在玩家根节点下。新增了 `HasActiveShieldOn` 静态检测，完美解决了“盾牌和身体 Trigger 判定顺序冲突导致挡弹仍掉血”的 Bug。
    *Shields are now attached directly to the player's root node. Added a static `HasActiveShieldOn` check, perfectly fixing the bug where "shield and body Trigger execution order conflict caused damage taken while blocking".*

### 3.2 无敌帧闪烁 / I-Frames Blink
*   修复了受击后高频闪烁失效的问题。
    *Fixed the issue where high-frequency blinking failed after taking a hit.*
*   将闪烁逻辑从“开关 Renderer”改回了“修改颜色的 Alpha 值（0 和 100 之间切换）”。
    *Reverted the blink logic from "toggling Renderer" back to "modifying color Alpha (switching between 0 and 100)".*
*   在 Inspector 中将 `Visible Duration` 和 `Hidden Duration` 调至 `0.05` 即可实现高频闪烁。
    *Set `Visible Duration` and `Hidden Duration` to `0.05` in the Inspector to achieve high-frequency blinking.*

## 4. 给数值策划的交接说明 / Handover Notes for Systems Designer

*   **玩家属性配置 (Player Stats Config)**：打开 Player 预制体，在 `PlayerStats` 脚本面板中可以直接配置初始的血量、移速、法强、护甲等。
    *Open the Player prefab. Initial health, speed, spell power, armor, etc., can be directly configured in the `PlayerStats` script panel.*
*   **地图道具制作 (Map Pickups Creation)**：制作“小豆豆”时，在触发拾取的代码中调用 `player.GetComponent<PlayerStats>().ModifyStat("属性英文名", 数值)` 即可。脚本内已写好详细的中英双语注释。
    *When creating "Pac-Man dots", simply call `player.GetComponent<PlayerStats>().ModifyStat("StatName", value)` in the pickup trigger code. Detailed bilingual comments are already written in the script.*
*   **法术数值配置 (Spell Stats Config)**：
    *   **冷却时间 (Cooldown)**：在对应的 `Spell Data` (ScriptableObject) 中修改。
        *Modify in the corresponding `Spell Data` (ScriptableObject).*
    *   **伤害/速度/异常状态 (Damage/Speed/Status Effects)**：在对应的法术预制体（如火球、陷阱）的 `SpellProjectile` 或 `HazardArea` 面板中直接修改。
        *Modify directly in the `SpellProjectile` or `HazardArea` panel of the corresponding spell prefab (e.g., fireball, trap).*
